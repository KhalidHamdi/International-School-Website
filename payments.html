Payments Information

Fees


The annual tuition fee is 50,000 LE. This amount covers the cost of education for one academic year.

Payment Calendar


The fees are divided into 3 installments:


  • First installment: August 1st

  • Second installment: November 1st

  • Third installment: February 1st

Parents and students are required to submit payments by the specified dates to ensure a smooth academic year.

Outstanding Scholarship Program

The Outstanding Scholarship Program aims to recognize and reward academic excellence, achievements, and contributions of students. The program offers various scholarships to eligible students based on different criteria. Here are some key details:


  • Academic Excellence Scholarship: Awarded to students with exceptional academic performance.

  • Leadership Scholarship: Recognizing students who have demonstrated outstanding leadership skills.

  • Community Involvement Scholarship: Awarded to students actively engaged in community service and volunteer work.

Eligibility criteria, application process, and deadlines for each scholarship category are available in the scholarship program guidelines. Students are encouraged to apply and showcase their achievements for an opportunity to receive financial support for their education.

Special Cases Fund Program

The Special Cases Fund Program is dedicated to providing financial assistance to students who may be facing unique challenges or special circumstances that impact their ability to meet educational expenses. The program aims to ensure that every student has the opportunity to receive quality education, regardless of financial constraints.


Key aspects of the Special Cases Fund Program:


  • Eligibility: Open to students facing financial hardships, emergencies, or unexpected situations affecting their ability to pay tuition and related fees.

  • Application Process: Students can apply for the Special Cases Fund by submitting a detailed application outlining their situation, financial need, and any supporting documentation.

  • Review Process: Applications will be reviewed by a committee to assess the validity of the request and determine the level of assistance needed.

  • Confidentiality: All information provided in the application process will be kept confidential to respect the privacy of the students and their families.

Students are encouraged to reach out to the school administration for more information about the Special Cases Fund Program, including the application process, eligibility criteria, and deadlines.
